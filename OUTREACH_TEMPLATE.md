## Product Hunt Comment Response Template

When someone asks "Why build this?":

```
Great question! I built this after deploying AI agents in production and realizing I had no visibility into what they were actually doing. 

Traditional monitoring tools don't work well for AI agents because:
1. AI decisions are non-deterministic
2. You need to understand the reasoning, not just the output
3. Compliance requirements need audit trails

OpenClaw solves this by logging every decision, creating checkpoints, and generating reports that auditors can understand.

Would love to hear what compliance/audit challenges you're facing with AI agents!
```
