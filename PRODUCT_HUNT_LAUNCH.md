## Tagline

## Description

**TL;DR:** OpenClaw Transparency Layer helps you understand what your AI agents are doing, create verification checkpoints, and generate compliance reports - all in one tool.

**Why we built this:**

As AI agents become more autonomous, they're making more decisions that affect our lives and businesses. But how do you know what they're actually doing? How do you audit their actions? How do you ensure compliance?

That's why we built OpenClaw Transparency Layer.

**What it does:**

🔍 **Action Tracking:** Log every decision your AI agent makes
✅ **Checkpoints:** Create verification points for critical actions
📊 **Compliance Reports:** Generate audit-ready documentation
🤝 **Multi-Agent Support:** Monitor multiple agents simultaneously
📈 **Advanced Visualization:** See patterns and anomalies
